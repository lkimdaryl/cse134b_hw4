About me

Hello there! I'm Kim, and I have a passion for crafting digital products on the web. My interest in computers began by the allure of video games. The mesmerizing graphics in these games inspired me, and propelled me to embark on a path of innovation and creativity.

Fast forward to today, I've had the privilege of collaborating with talented student-led teams at the University of California San Diego.
